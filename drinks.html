CAIPIRINHA

Ingredients

    300 ml ginger ale 120 ml passion fruit juice 4 limes 8 teaspoons of cane sugar Mint to garnish Crushed Ice

Method

First, wash the limes and cut each in 8 pieces. Fill the cane sugar into two glasses, add the limes and crush them. Now add the passion fruit juice and stir well. Finally, add the crushed ice and the ginger ale and garnish your mocktail with some mint!
