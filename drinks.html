Classic Old Fashioned

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons simple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon water
  • 2 dashes bitter
  • 1 cup ice cubes
  • 1 jigger bourbon
  • 1 slice orange
  • 1 maraschino cherry

Method

Pour the simple syrup, water, bitters into a whiskey glass. Stir to combine, then place the ice cubes in the glass. Pour bourbon over the ice and garnish with the prange slice and maraschino cherry.

SIMPLE MOSCOW MULE

Ingredients

  • ice
  • 1/2 fresh lime
  • 2 fluid ounces vodka
  • 4 fluid ounces ginger beer, or to taste
  • 2 lime slices

Method

Fill a tall glass with ice. Squeeze 1/2 lime over ice. Pour vodka over ice and top with ginger beer. Garnish with lime slices.
